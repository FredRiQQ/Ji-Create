Ji-Create Platform

Ji-Create is a creative economy platform designed to help artists, creators, and cultural innovators monetize their craft through storytelling, live engagement, and direct access to their audience.

On Ji-Create, creatives are more than content producers, they are brands. Artists can sell their art and crafts alongside the stories behind them, connect with fans through live streaming, and earn directly from their talent.

With built-in backend tools, creators gain access to audience insights, performance tracking, and monetization analytics, giving them full control over their creative business.

Key Features

  • Sell art, crafts,tickets and digital creations with storytelling
  • Live streaming for performances and fan engagement
  • Artist discovery and promotion by event organizers
  • Merchandise sales and fan support
  • Creator dashboard with audience & revenue tracking
